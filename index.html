About WAFFLICON

Founded with a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences, WAFFLICON is your go-to destination for indie game development and creative merchandise. Our mission is to blend technical excellence with artistic vision.

What We Do

Software Development: We craft custom software solutions, web applications, and indie games that prioritize user experience and clean code.

Game Development: From concept to completion, we develop indie games that tell stories and create memorable experiences.

Merchandise: Our t-shirt and merch line celebrates developer culture with unique designs that speak to the creative coding community.

Our Story

WAFFLICON started as a passion project combining love for programming, game development, and creative expression. The waffle/grid concept represents the structured yet creative nature of code - organized, purposeful, but infinitely customizable.

Our Mission

We believe in creating digital experiences that matter. Whether it's a game that tells a compelling story, software that solves real problems, or merchandise that brings the developer community together, everything we create is designed with purpose and passion.
